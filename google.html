The most popular search engine, Google is belongs to Alphabet, which is a collection of different companies. In Google's projects, the largest share of revenue comes from advertising. Whenever people use the Google search engine, the entire system analyzes user preferences based on search history, IP location, time spent on each page, and other factors. Based on these calculations, the Google engine automatically inserts targeted ads onto the user's search interface. Behind every ad we see on Google may lie billions of dollars in investment from the company. At the same time, Google also charges service fees for certain specific features. These two components constitute Google's largest revenue streams.
